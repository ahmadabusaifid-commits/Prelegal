# Prelegal

A platform for drafting common legal agreements, making it easy to create, customize, and manage legal documents without specialized legal knowledge.

## Features

- Draft common legal agreements (NDAs, service contracts, employment agreements, etc.)
- Customizable templates tailored to your needs
- Simple, guided workflow for document creation
- Export-ready documents
